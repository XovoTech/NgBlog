There are no doubt many blogging websites like Medium, Dev.to, and many more.

So, why bother creating another blog website?

As a developer, I really love to read amazing articles and content from amazing peoples. But the problem everyone faces is the limited access. Medium has restriction for free user to view 2 contents per day. This limits the user to read amazing articles and sometimes leads to frustration in my case.

My Goal is to allow people to post content and help them analyze their reach. This is not a proper blog platform at the moment but will do hope that your feedback contributes to make it a proper blog platform for users to read through.
